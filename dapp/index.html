Degen Vibes?

Planet Degen is dying. The inhabitants of the planet have neglected the environment and used up natural resources at an unsustainable rate for years. Climate change has caused coastal flooding, forests have become deserts, temperatures continue to rise, and air quality has become almost unbreathable.

Most species of life have suffered from mass extinction. The only known surviving inhabitants are 3,333 Degens. But the Degen Vibes cannot survive for long on the surface. Their only hope is to descend, one-by-one, to the Photo Labs, the OG CLUB. The only entrance to the DegenVibes CLUB, deep below the planet's surface, is via an elevator that has space for only one Degen at a time.

As time passes, more and more Degens will join the CLUB, forming a DAA. Together the Degens must plan for the survival of the species. How to best use the limited supply of GOOD VIBES to sustain themselves? Can they develop tools and accessories to reverse climate change and heal the planet? Or should they develop spaceships and related technology, to embark on a space voyage in search of a new home? And could the rumors be true, that some of the Ohmies have managed to survive as well? Members of the Degen Vibes CLUB will have to decide where they go from here...

Degen Vibes combine NFTs with DeFi. The degenerative art is inspired by Cryptopunks. The DAO and treasury is inspired by Nouns. The DeFi and money streams aim to explore a new community that collides the worlds of NFTs and DeFi into a new type of degen. Grab your Degen and join the Club.

Code based on Degen Dogs, a finalist and winner at the ETHOnline Hackathon, and is powered by Superfluid, Uniswap, Idle Finance, Tally, and soon JonesDAO & Dopex Vaults with OHM. The Dapp is modified from Nounds DAO, as is the Auction House contract, which was itself modified from the Zora Auction House contract.

Auctions

Degen Vibes are sold via auction, with only a single Degen being auction at any time. Bids are submitted on the Polygon network in WETH (Polygon Wrapped Ether, we'll deploy on testnet first!). When each auction ends, the auction is settled and the Degen is transferred to the winning bidder.

Auction details can be found here.

Ohmly Degens?

The WETH proceeds from Degen Vibes auction are immediately and automatically invested in DeFi and start earning yield for Degen owners. The WETH is invested in the "Best Yield" vault on Idle Finance. (and soon OHM vaults?) Idle tokens representing the vault shares are issued to Degen Vibes contract. Distribution of proceeds is as follows:

  • 50% for the past, 50% for the future
  • 50% is streamed back to past Degen owners
  • 10% is immediately donated to a non-profit DAO
  • The remaining 40% goes to the treasury, where it is controlled by the DAO.

Tree Tree

Degen Vibes #1, the very first auction, will feature a special "One Tree For the Planet" body trait, and will be the only Degen with this trait. 100% of the proceeds of this auction will be donated to OTP. This special auction will last 72 hours.

VIBES Tokens

VIBES -- are ERC20 tokens issued to all bidders of Degen Vibes auctions, including both unsuccessful and winning bidders. VIBES tokens are issued at a ratio of 1000:1 based on the bid submitted. For example, a bid of 1 WETH would result in 1000 VIBES tokens.

More details can be found here.

Degen Vibes DAO

Degen Vibes owners are members of the DAO: one Degen equals one vote. DAO members control the treasury and decide the future of Degen Vibes. (more info)

Degenerative Artwork

The artwork for Degen Vibes was inspired by the pixel art of Cryptopunks. The degenerative art combines 12 Degen body types with various accessories such as glasses, hats, and more. Some traits are more rare than others. There are 3,333 known Degen Vibes, each with a unique combination of traits.
